Intro

Tsinghua University, located in Haidian District, Beijing, is a state key university directly under the direct administration of the Ministry of Education of the People's Republic of China. It is known as the "cradle of Red Engineers" was founded in 1911. When the People's Republic of China was founded in 1949, Tsinghua University entered a new stage of development. 1952 The department was adjusted to become a multi-subject technical university. Since 1978, it has been gradually restored and developed into a comprehensive research university.

Intro

Peking University was founded in 1898 at the time of the Reform and Reform, and was originally named the Peking University Hall. 1912 It was renamed National Peking University. Peking University is the center of the New Culture Movement and the source of the May Fourth Movement. It was the first to spread Marxism and scientific and democratic thought in China, and is one of the important bases for the founding of the Communist Party of China. Over the years, Peking University has always shared a common destiny with China and the Chinese people, and moved forward with The Times and society.

Intro

Beijing University of Posts and Telecommunications ,founded in 1955,is the first institution of higher learning of posts and telecommunications in the People's Republic of China. It belongs to the former Ministry of Posts and Telecommunications. Located in Beijing, it is a national key university under the direct administration of the Ministry of Education of the People's Republic of China and co-built by the Ministry of Industry and Information Technology. It is one of the national "Double first-class" universities, "Project 211" and "Project 985 Superior Discipline Innovation Platform". It is also a founding member of Beijing High-tech University Alliance, China Artificial Intelligence Education Alliance and CDIO Engineering Education Alliance.

Intro

Beihang University was founded in 1952 by merging the departments of aeronautics and astronautics of Tsinghua University, Beiyang University, Xiamen University, Sichuan University and Chongqing University. In 1959, Beihang University was designated as A national key university. In 2017, Beihang University was selected into the Class A list of "World-class universities and first-class disciplines". It is a national key university directly under the Ministry of Industry and Information Technology, PRC and a university directly under the central government. It is one of the national "Double first-class", "Project 985" and "Project 211" key universities.

Intro

The predecessor of Beijing Normal University was founded in 1902 as the Normal College of the Capital University. In 1923, it was renamed Beijing Normal University, becoming the first normal university in China. In 1931 and 1952, Beiping Women's Normal University and Furen University were merged into Beijing Normal University. It is a national key university directly under the direct administration of the Ministry of Education of the People's Republic of China and jointly built by the Ministry of Education and Beijing Municipality. It is ranked among the "Double first-class", "Project 985" and "Project 211".
