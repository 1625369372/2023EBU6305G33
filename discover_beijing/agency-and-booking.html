Intro

Tsinghua University, located in Haidian District, Beijing, is a state key university directly under the direct administration of the Ministry of Education of the People's Republic of China. It is known as the "cradle of Red Engineers" was founded in 1911. When the People's Republic of China was founded in 1949, Tsinghua University entered a new stage of development. 1952 The department was adjusted to become a multi-subject technical university. Since 1978, it has been gradually restored and developed into a comprehensive research university.
