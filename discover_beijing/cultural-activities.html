Cultural Activities

Xiangshan Park

Xiangshan is a forest park with royal garden features in the western suburbs of Beijing. It is a favorite place for Beijing citizens to enjoy autumn leaves and exercise by climbing. The park has a long history and many cultural relics. Its abundant forest resources and mountainous terrain have also been rated as one of the "New Sixteen Scenic Spots of Beijing.Every year during the Spring Festival, Xiangshan Park will prepare 10 colorful activities such as traditional culture, welcome flower exhibition, red tour, popular science tour, etc., to welcome the Spring Festival in Xiangshan with tourists.

Admire Flowers

Enjoying flowers in Beijing is different from other cities. You can see beautiful flowers, enjoy the ancient charm in the flowers, and taste the ancient things in the ancient charm.The Ming City Wall Ruins Park is famous for its plum blossoms; The spring flowers of Yulan, Yanjing Yoshino Sakura, and Haitang in Longtan West Lake Park have bloomed one after another; The peach, green peach, apricot plum, and crabapple in the Lianhua River Urban Leisure Park also have a unique taste; The Olympic Forest Park has the largest flower appreciation area in Beijing. The spring flower varieties in the park include peach, elm leaf plum, forsythia, apricot, lilac, various colored crabapple, green peach, cherry blossom, etc. There are many varieties, a large number, and a long viewing period, making it the best choice for nearby residents to visit and appreciate flowers.The Ming City Wall Ruins Park is famous for its plum blossoms; The spring flowers of Yulan, Yanjing Yoshino Sakura, and Haitang in Longtan West Lake Park have bloomed one after another; The peach, green peach, apricot plum, and crabapple in the Lianhua River Urban Leisure Park also have a unique taste; The Olympic Forest Park has the largest flower appreciation area in Beijing. Beijing Long Distance Running Festival

More than 50 years ago, in order to promote the development of sports and expand sports promotion, major newspapers jointly launched the Beijing Victory Cup Round the City Race, which was the original origin of the Beijing International Long Distance Running Festival and an important traditional brand event in Beijing.
