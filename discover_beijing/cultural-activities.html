Cultural Activities

Xiangshan is a forest park with royal garden features in the western suburbs of Beijing. It is a favorite place for Beijing citizens to enjoy autumn leaves and exercise by climbing. The park has a long history and many cultural relics. Its abundant forest resources and mountainous terrain have also been rated as one of the "New Sixteen Scenic Spots of Beijing.
