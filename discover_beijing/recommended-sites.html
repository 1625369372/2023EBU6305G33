The Palace Museum

The Palace Museum in Beijing is a comprehensive museum established on October 10, 1925. It is a large comprehensive museum of ancient art established on the basis of the Ming and Qing imperial palaces and their collections. It is also the largest museum of ancient Chinese culture and art.
